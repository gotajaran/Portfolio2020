Matching the crisis intervention experience for third party supporters.

Overview

Redesigning the crisis intervention experience for caregivers.

Company Profile

Suicide is Different (SID) offers crisis intervention education for people who want to help.

The Problem

Users were not learning through the intended 5-step module.

The Outcome

Improved crisis intervention experience, updated UI and developed on Squarespace.

My Role

Product Manager, Lead Designer, and
Squarespace Developer

My Team

1 Researcher and 2 Client Stakeholders.

My Contribution

Collaborated on user research, designed wireframes, prototypes, UI, and development.

Summary

SID provides crisis intervention education for people who want to support others.

SID's value proposition is that they focus on the touch points that occur after the initial crisis. Oftentimes, someone experiencing a crisis may go through a similar predicament in the future. SID provides the tools and resources for those who want to provide long-time support.

The challenge for this project was thinking about how can we map someone's experience of navigating this crisis live, and what tools or resources will be most relevant for them. The outcome of this project was a complete IA overhaul and accentuating the existing visual brand.

A more detailed case study coming soon. In the meantime feel free the check out the site, it's live: suicideisdifferent.org.
